About me

Hey there! I'm Anurag Pratap Singh, a passionate freelancer immersed in the world of video editing. I specialize in wielding the creative powers of software like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and CapCut, promising my clients not just what they expect (x), but always delivering something extra (x+2).
The feedback from my last client speaks volumes –
"Anurag has taken a 360-degree leap from my latest video."

Beyond the editing suite, I'm an engineering undergrad with a diverse range of experiences.I've worn the hat of a product intern in three startups, blending technical know-how with creative flair. Currently, I've taken the plunge into full-time video editing, crafting mostly explainer shorts for clients eager to ride the wave of short-form content.
But don't let that fool you – I'm equally adept at weaving cinematic and long-form videos.
I'm not just an editor; I'm a storyteller, an innovator, and my name is synonymous with transformative visual experiences. Let's create something extraordinary together!
